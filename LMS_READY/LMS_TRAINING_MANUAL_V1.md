## 🛡️ MODULE 1: OHS ACT SECTION 8 & EXECUTIVE LIABILITY

### 1.1 South African OHS Act Section 8 (General Duties)
The Occupational Health and Safety (OHS) Act Section 8(1) mandates that every employer shall provide and maintain, as far as is reasonably practicable, a working environment that is safe and without risk to the health of their employees.

*   **2026 Legal Standard**: The Department of Employment and Labour (DEL) formalizes that hybrid home-offices and distributed environments constitute recognized workplaces. Employers are legally required to audit and manage the physical (ergonomic) and psychosocial (cognitive strain) safety of remote workers.
*   **Reasonably Practicable**: A digital audit and fatigue-gate framework (like ErgoSafe) serves as an officially admissible compliance ledger, documenting active risk management.

### 1.2 Section 37 & 38 (Vicarious Liability & Penalties)
*   **Section 37 (Vicarious Liability)**: Establishes that the employer/executive board is vicariously liable for any injuries, ergonomic strain, or psychosocial incidents sustained by employees working remotely.
*   **Section 38(1) Penalties**: Administrative fines up to **10% of annual turnover** or **R5,000,000** (whichever is greater), alongside criminal prosecution and up to 2 years imprisonment for negligent directors.

## ⚠️ MODULE 2: WORKPLACE RISK IDENTIFICATION PROTOCOLS

### 2.1 The 5-Step Risk Assessment Method
All SHE representatives and forecourt managers must actively deploy the 5-step hazard assessment cycle:
1.  **Identify Hazards**: Document all physical, cognitive, ergonomic, and chemical hazards.
2.  **Analyze Who/What is at Risk**: Identify affected personnel (attendants, branch administrators, WFH operators).
3.  **Evaluate Risk and Calculate Rating**: Apply the probability and severity matrix.
4.  **Implement Controls**: Enforce controls (Elimination, Substitution, Engineering, Administration, PPE).
5.  **Review and Record**: Log continuous telemetry inside the local database.

### 2.2 Risk Rating Score Matrix
$$\text{Risk Score} = \text{Probability (1-5)} \times \text{Severity (1-5)}$$

*   **1 - 4 (Low)**: Standard administrative controls; monitor periodically.
*   **5 - 12 (Medium)**: Operational controls required; schedule ergonomic adjustments.
*   **15 - 25 (High/Extreme)**: Immediate shutdown/lockout; trigger Nelly's Fatigue-Gate or forecourt evacuation.

## 🛢️ MODULE 3: FIRST AID & EMERGENCY RESPONSE SEQUENCES

### 3.1 Forecourt HazMat Petroleum Spill Sequence
In service station environments (Engen, BP, Shell, Astron Energy), attendants must execute emergency sequences in under 60 seconds:
1.  **Emergency Stop**: Trigger the Emergency Shutdown Valve (ESD) to cut off fuel pumps.
2.  **Isolate & Evacuate**: Direct customers away from the zone, extinguish all ignition sources, and seal the area.
3.  **Containment**: Deploy dry-chemical powder (DCP) and spill sorbents (peat/silica) to capture run-off. Prevent petroleum from entering general stormwater drainage to avoid environmental penalties.
4.  **Incident Reporting**: Log the incident under Section 24 of the OHS Act using the digital dashboard.

### 3.2 First Aid Response Sequence (DRSABCD)
*   **D**anger: Assess immediate surroundings for safety.
*   **R**esponse: Check casualty for responsiveness.
*   **S**end: Send for medical assistance.
*   **A**irway: Clear and open airway.
*   **B**reathing: Check for normal breathing.
*   **C**PR: Start CPR (30 compressions to 2 breaths) if unresponsive and not breathing.
*   **D**efibrillation: Attach AED if available.

## 🧠 MODULE 4: PSYCHOSOCIAL SAFETY & THE RIGHT TO DISCONNECT

### 4.1 CCMA "Digital Tethering" and Unpaid Overtime
In 2026, CCMA constructive dismissal and unpaid overtime claims due to after-hours messaging have risen by **142%**. Remote and hybrid workers are protected from digital exhaustion under the ISO 45003:2021 standard.

### 4.2 Active Fatigue-Gate Mitigation
To protect the company from vicarious liability:
*   **Fatigue-Gate Lockouts**: Restrict late-night commits and out-of-bounds operations.
*   **Cognitive Handshakes**: Force interactive screen breaks and micro-stretches, logging the employee's active compliance status. This generates an executive shield, proving the company took all practicable measures to prevent psychosocial burnout.
